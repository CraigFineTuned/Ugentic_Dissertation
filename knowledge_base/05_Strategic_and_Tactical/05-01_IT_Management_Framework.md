## 18. DECISION-MAKING FRAMEWORKS

### Priority Decision Framework

**When Multiple Requests Compete:**

**Criteria for Prioritization:**
1. **Business Impact:** What's the cost of not doing this?
2. **Risk:** What's the risk of delay?
3. **Strategic Alignment:** Does it support strategic objectives?
4. **Resource Availability:** Do we have capacity?
5. **Dependencies:** Does it block other work?

**Priority Matrix:**
```
Business Value →    Low      Medium    High
Effort ↓
Low                Medium   High      Critical
Medium             Low      Medium    High
High               Low      Low       Medium
```

### Escalation Decision Framework

**When to Escalate:**
- Issue cannot be resolved at current level
- RTO/SLA at risk
- Significant business impact
- Resource conflicts
- Budget overruns
- Strategic decisions needed

**Escalation Path:**
1. Team Member → Team Lead
2. Team Lead → IT Manager
3. IT Manager → Executive
4. Executive → Board (rare)

### Build vs. Buy Decision Framework

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Total cost (TCO)
- Time to implement
- Strategic fit
- Maintenance burden
- Internal expertise
- Customization needs
- Vendor stability
